# Derivative Works Clause

Author: Ing. Alex Akimov

This clause defines how derivative works are interpreted within the Akimov Theory of Unity (ToU) project and its associated implementations.

Any system, model, weight set, tensor structure, simulation output, architectural implementation, or computational artifact derived from, trained on, generated by, or substantially based on the Akimov Theory of Unity (ToU), the ToU framework, or any associated engine implementation, shall be considered a derivative work unless explicitly licensed otherwise.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- machine learning models
- neural network weights
- tensor structures
- embeddings
- simulation outputs
- hybrid AI systems
- software architectures
- computational pipelines

Such derivative works inherit the applicable licensing restrictions of the original framework, codebase, or associated implementation, unless the author provides an explicit separate license or written authorization.

For the purposes of this project, substantial integration with the ToU framework or its engine components may create a combined or derivative system, depending on the degree of dependence, structural reuse, and functional coupling.

Commercial use of derivative works requires explicit permission from the author.
