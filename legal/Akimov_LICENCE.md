Akimov License Framework (ALF v1.0)

Author: Ing. Alex Akimov

This framework defines the licensing structure governing the Akimov Theory of Unity (ToU), its implementations, and derivative works.

---

1. Scientific Publications (Zenodo)
Licensed under CC BY-NC 4.0

---

2. Research Use (GitHub / PUBLIC)
Permitted for non-commercial and academic purposes only.

---

3. Derivative Works
Any derivative works, including but not limited to:
- models
- tensors
- weights
- simulations
- system architectures

are subject to the same licensing conditions unless explicitly authorized.

---

4. Engine and Code
Specific implementations (e.g., ToU Engine) may be subject to stronger copyleft or custom licensing.

---

5. Commercial Use
Any commercial use requires explicit permission and licensing from the author.

---

6. Enforcement
Unauthorized use may result in legal action.
