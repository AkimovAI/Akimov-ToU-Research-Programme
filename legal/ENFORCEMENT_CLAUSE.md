# Enforcement and Violation Clause

Author: Ing. Alex Akimov

This clause defines the enforcement position of the Akimov Theory of Unity (ToU) project and its associated legal framework.

Any unauthorized use, reproduction, commercial exploitation, derivative implementation, model training, system integration, or redistribution of the Akimov Theory of Unity (ToU), its framework, engine implementations, or associated materials, in violation of the applicable license terms, shall be considered a breach of the project’s licensing conditions.

Such breach may trigger one or more of the following responses:

- revocation of granted usage rights
- demand for immediate cessation of unauthorized use
- request for removal of infringing materials
- formal legal notice
- claim for damages where applicable
- requirement to obtain a commercial or corrective license

The author reserves the right to distinguish between:
- accidental misuse
- negligent misuse
- deliberate infringement
- commercial exploitation without authorization

Nothing in this clause limits the author’s right to pursue legal remedies under applicable copyright, licensing, contractual, or commercial law.

This clause exists to preserve the integrity, authorship, and lawful use of the ToU framework and its associated implementations.
