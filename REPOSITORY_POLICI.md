Its purpose is to provide:
- public research visibility
- structured documentation
- reproducible reference points
- integrity-preserving archival records

Public visibility does not imply unrestricted commercial use.

- research review
- educational reading
- scholarly analysis
- open technical inspection
- reproducible discussion
